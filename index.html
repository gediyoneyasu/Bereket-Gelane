About Me

Hello, my name is Bereket Gelane. I am an Ethiopian trader, passionate investor, and dedicated volunteer worker based in Gessa, Ethiopia. With years of experience in local and regional markets, I specialize in creating sustainable business opportunities, supporting small-scale entrepreneurs, and uplifting communities through volunteer initiatives. My mission is to bridge modern trade practices with grassroots development, ensuring mutual growth and prosperity across Ethiopia.
